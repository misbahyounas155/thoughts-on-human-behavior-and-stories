# Free Will vs Fate

The question of free will and fate explores one of the biggest mysteries of human existence.

We do not choose where we are born, our early experiences, or many circumstances that shape our lives. These factors influence the direction of our journey.

However, we still make choices about how we respond.

Maybe fate creates the situation, but our decisions shape our character. Human beings have emotions, tendencies, and limitations, but they also have the ability to reflect and choose.

The balance between fate and free will may exist in the space between what happens to us and what we decide to do next.

We may not control every chapter of life, but we still participate in writing the story.
